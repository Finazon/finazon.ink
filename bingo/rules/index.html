Offical race Results

Updated By: Finazon

Game: Splatoon 3 - RotM

1: No using websites/photos during the race.

2: No help from others during the race.

3: No skips the allow you to get to a new site without clearing the ooze in front of the pipe (EX: moxy skip or owen skip).

4: Lines required to win are determined by the players (or lockout).

5: The Start of races is the DJ Octavio Fight OR at the profile select screen - The DJ Octavio fight is the standard.

6: For goals that say beat all levels using [weapon], you MUST use the listen weapon for that level

Note: Cutscene skips and the rocket levels 2 and 3 skips are allowed.
